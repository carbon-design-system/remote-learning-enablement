## IBM Enterprise Design Thinking

  </Column>
  <Column colMd={6} colLg={8}>

IBM Enterprise Design Thinking is a framework for human-centered problem solving. Grounded in empathy and collaboration, it’s an engaging way for teachers to activate groups of students in a virtual class setting. IBM’s online resources include video learning modules and a toolkit of activities designed for dispersed groups to collaborate together around a human problem. We’ve made these IBM Enterprise Design Thinking resources available for free.

[Learn more about IBM Enterprise Design Thinking](https://www.ibm.com/design/thinking/)
